    About Me:

    I wanted to introduce myself better and talk more about my drawings, ideas and projects. I've liked drawing since I was 11 years old or so, but I started getting better at it at 12 years old. Now that I'm 16 y/o, I'm trying to make some money with my work and to have some fun while doing it. Drawing helps me get distracted and focus on something nice which I know will lead me to a better future. Drawing, art and programing have been my ways of escaping reality and of avoiding to pay too much attention to anxiety, which may come often sometimes. Although I'm really into drawing and programming, I still have one big issue which limits me from doing these things: time.
    I don't always have the time to make significant progress on my art, really. Remember to always take into account the fact that I spend 8:30hs at school per day, which is extremely tiring. Besides, I'm not even mentioning all the hours I spend studying at home (doing homework or studying for tests). It's really tyring to have this lifestyle and still go to the gym, still hang out with friends and still try to make a progress with my art. As you can see, my resting time is hardly ever enough haha
